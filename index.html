Ahoy!

You have reached Alexis, and on paper I am a Computer Science student studying at the University of British Columbia. In real life, however, I have a much more difficult time labelling myself.

I'm 1/3 of the indie-folk group, The Calliopes, and also a solo artist, Alexis the Artless. I'm a theatre kid who will never get tired of the drama, and after a brief stint in the English Honours program, I have finally found my way over to the techy side, because I heard they have cookies (get it, like browser cookies).

Anyways, as a wise instagrammer (@bymariandrew) once said, "leading a passionate life doesn't mean finding ONE THING that is the center of your life", because WE are the centers of our lives, and "all our interests and curiosities can float around us like good company and consistent friends". For me, some of these passions can be seen in the opening animation.

Some more of my interests lie in the creative applications of technology: in particular, iOS Development, Virtual Reality, and Game Development. There are so many possibilities in these fields (i.e. Immersive Journalism, pioneered by Nonny de la Pena), and I can't wait to be part of the movement that stretches the limits of this tech even further.


Interested in working together? Give me a holler!
